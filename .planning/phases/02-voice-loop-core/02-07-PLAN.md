  <name>Task 3: iPad end-to-end voice loop validation</name>
  <what-built>
Complete voice loop: tap → ListeningMode (blue waveform, Deepgram STT) → ThinkingMode (blue→purple orb) → SpeakingMode (purple waveform + subtitle TTS) → idle. Conversation history in MongoDB. Russian + English support.
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
On iPad in standalone PWA mode (added to home screen — NOT Safari browser):

**Test 1: English voice loop (baseline)**
1. Open JARVIS PWA from iPad home screen (fullscreen, no browser chrome)
2. Tap the orb/screen
3. Say clearly: "What is the capital of France?"
4. VERIFY: Blue waveform appears and reacts to your voice (LIST-02)
5. VERIFY: After 1.5-2s silence, waveform disappears and thinking orb appears (THINK-01)
6. VERIFY: Orb transitions from blue to purple during thinking (THINK-02)
7. VERIFY: Speaking mode appears with purple waveform and subtitle text (SPEAK-01, SPEAK-02)
8. VERIFY: JARVIS says "Paris" or similar — audible speech plays (TTS-01)
9. VERIFY: After speaking ends, returns to idle orb screen

**Test 2: Russian language**
1. Tap screen and say in Russian: "Какая столица Франции?"
2. VERIFY: Response is in Russian (CONV-02)
3. VERIFY: Russian voice (Milena or similar) is used if available (TTS-02)

**Test 3: Conversation history**
1. Ask: "Tell me about Paris"
2. After JARVIS responds, tap and ask: "What is its most famous landmark?"
3. VERIFY: JARVIS responds about Paris's landmark (proves CONV-03 — history context used)

**Test 4: Tap to stop**
1. Ask a long question to get a long response
2. While JARVIS is speaking, tap the screen
3. VERIFY: Speech stops immediately and returns to idle (TTS-03)

**Test 5: Silence detection timing**
1. Tap and say: "Um..." then pause
2. VERIFY: Recording stops after approximately 1.5-2 seconds (VOICE-04)
3. VERIFY: Short pause mid-sentence does NOT cut off speech prematurely

If any test FAILS, note: which test, what was observed, what was expected.
